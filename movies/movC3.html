Bride of Chucky

Child's Play (also known as Chucky) is a horror franchise centered on a series of seven films created by Don Mancini. Primarily, the films focus on Chucky (played by Brad Dourif), a notorious serial killer known as the "Lakeshore Strangler", who before he dies, transfers his soul into a harmless doll from the fictional "Good Man" brand.
